# MCS-150 Biennial Update

**Domain:** compliance | **CFR:** 49 CFR § 390.19 | **Updated:** 2026-06-16

## Filing Requirement

Motor carriers must file an **MCS-150** (Motor Carrier Identification Report) and update biennially based on the last two digits of the DOT number:

| DOT ends in | File in odd or even year |
|------------|-------------------------|
| Odd (1,3,5,7,9) | Odd-numbered years |
| Even (0,2,4,6,8) | Even-numbered years |

## What to Update

- Legal name, DBA, and physical address
- Number of power units and drivers
- Operation classification (interstate/intrastate, cargo type)
- Contact information (critical for MOTUS PIN delivery)

## Filing Methods

1. **Online** via FMCSA MOTUS portal (preferred, fastest)
2. **Paper** Form MCS-150 mailed to FMCSA (slower, use when locked out of MOTUS)

## Penalties for Non-Compliance

Failure to update can result in deactivation of USDOT number and OOS orders. A deactivated DOT blocks new contracts on verified load boards.

## Military Time Tip

Schedule MCS-150 updates during off-peak portal hours (before **0600** or after **2000** Eastern) to avoid MOTUS timeout errors during business hours.
