# FMCSA Insurance Requirements

**Domain:** compliance | **CFR:** 49 CFR Part 387 | **Updated:** 2026-06-18

## Minimum Liability Coverage (Property Carrier)

| Freight Type | Minimum Coverage |
|-------------|-----------------|
| General freight | $750,000 |
| Household goods | $750,000 |
| Oil / hazardous (non-bulk) | $1,000,000 |
| Hazardous materials (bulk) | $5,000,000 |

Brokers must maintain a **$75,000 BMC-84 surety bond** or trust fund (BMC-85) — not the same as carrier liability insurance.

## Filing Forms

- **BMC-91 / BMC-91X** — Liability insurance filing by insurer
- **BMC-34** — Cargo insurance (if applicable)
- **Form MCS-90** — Endorsement for liability policy

Insurance must be filed electronically through the insurer's process; FMCSA will not activate authority without valid filings on record.

## Cancellation Rules

Insurers must give FMCSA **35 days' notice** before cancelling a policy. Authority becomes inactive if coverage lapses — carriers may be placed Out of Service (OOS).

## laslogTMX Verification Tie-In

Companies on the internal load board must be **laslogTMX Verified** with current insurance visible on SAFER. Brokers should verify carrier insurance status before awarding loads.
