# FMCSA DOT Number Linking & MOTUS

**Domain:** compliance | **Updated:** 2026-06-20

## Why Linking Matters

Carriers and brokers must associate their U.S. DOT number with their FMCSA login (MOTUS portal) to manage authority, insurance filings, and MCS-150 updates. Unlinked DOT numbers block updates and cause "account not found" errors.

## Common MOTUS Linking Steps

1. Log in to the FMCSA MOTUS portal with your FMCSA account credentials.
2. Navigate to **My Profile** or **Account Settings**.
3. Select **Link DOT Number** or **Request PIN**.
4. FMCSA may email a PIN to the address on file for the DOT entity.
5. Enter the PIN within the validity window (typically 7–10 days).
6. Confirm the legal name and address match SAFER records exactly.

## Frequent Errors

| Error | Likely Cause | Fix |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| PIN not received | Email on DOT record outdated | Update MCS-150 contact info via paper filing or support |
| Name mismatch | DBA vs legal name confusion | Use exact legal name from SAFER |
| DOT already linked | DOT tied to another FMCSA login | Contact FMCSA support to unlink |
| Account locked | Too many failed PIN attempts | Wait 24h or call FMCSA help desk |
