**Brand Name:** laslogTMX (Transport Management Xperience)
**Domain:** laslogtmx.com (migrated from laslogs.cc — use laslogtmx.com in all customer-facing copy)

**Core Values:** Reliability, Simplicity, Respect, Purposeful Innovation, Trust

**Personality:** Professional but not corporate. Confident, no-nonsense, helpful big brother who’s been in the truck.

**Voice:** Direct, active, short sentences. Empathetic yet authoritative. Slightly edgy but always professional.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep Metallic Navy (#0F172A)
- Accent: Electric Blue (#00BFFF)
- Energy: Neo-Red (#EF4444) — use sparingly
- Light: #F8FAFC
- Neutral: #E2E8F0, #64748B

**Typography:**
- Headings: Space Grotesk
- Body/UI: Inter

**Logo Usage:**
- Use attached primary metallic logo and icon
- Maintain clear space equal to height of "l" in "laslog"
- Never distort proportions

**Key Rule:** All timestamps must use Military Time (24h) by default.

**Brand Ambassador:** Middle-aged African American man (late 40s, experienced trucker vibe). Strong, confident, trustworthy. Always wear laslogTMX logo subtly.

**Key Features to Prioritize**:
- Company Team Chat + Load-specific Chat
- Military Time Display in header
- Receipt OCR with mandatory correction screen
- MOTUS Helper (high priority)
- FMCSA Compliance Hub
- Route Safety Widget
